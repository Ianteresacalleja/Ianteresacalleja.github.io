Private Eye - Mystery novella subscription box

Private Eye is a themed subscription box service delivering a monthly, curated selection of novellas, merchandise, and reading materials tailored to each subscriber's interests.

The visual language draws from film noir and Art Deco design, alongside the work of Agatha Christie, and games such as Clue and L.A. Noire.

Gutter - Punk streetwear

Gutter is an activism-focused streetwear brand inspired by 1970s and 80s punk aesthetics.

The identity embraces ruggedness and explosive individuality without taking itself too seriously. Its mascot, a rat named Gutter, presents himself as a small-scale revolutionary, wearing a tin can helmet, raising his fist, and wielding a safety pin as protection.
