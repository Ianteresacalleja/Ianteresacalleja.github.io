miami zine fair

The Miami Zine Fair is an annual event in the Design District showcasing zines by local artists. I was given full creative freedom on this project.

I initially began with a poster that abstracted the form of a zine into a mosaic-like composition.

As I continued developing the materials, I shifted toward a more figurative approach to better engage my classmates, embracing color, fluid geometry, and anatomy, inspired by the Miami art scene.
